Sneak into Basket

As users buy something, the site sneaks an extra thing into the user's shopping basket. This is accomplished utilizing opt-in/opt-out choices that are checked of course. Now and then, when taking the thing off the user's cart, the user needs to go to a particular page to eliminate that thing.

cellularoutfitter.com

I don’t really know this company so I’m going to tell its story exactly as darkatterns.org describes it.

When you add 'Premium Ultra Edge Sturdy Shockproof Bumper Transparent Case, Black/Clear For Galaxy A51 5G' and go to cart page. In the cart page Cellular Outfitter Universal Expandable Phone Holder, Black that costs $3.99 has been added to the cart automatically. If the customer don't notice and uncheck this then the product will be ordered.
