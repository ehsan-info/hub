Who runs the site?

Do you want to participate in the initiative?

Use social sites to spread the word about Dark Patterns. It's a really effective way to put pressure onto companies to stop using Dark Patterns.

  • Retweet, quote and favourite other people's tweets about Dark Patterns.
  • @mention the offending brands and tell them what you think about their practices.
  • When you see a Dark Pattern, take screenshots and tweet it.
  • If you've got an example that doesn't fit into a tweet, you can use a platform like Medium, then tweet it.
  • Mention @darkpatterns or use the hashtag #darkpattern.
